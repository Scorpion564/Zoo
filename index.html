Jamshedpur Zoo

Tata Steel Zoological Park

Tata Zoological Park, also known as Tata Steel Zoological Park, is a popular wildlife destination located in Jamshedpur. The park spans over an area of 172 acres and houses a variety of animals, including tigers, lions, bears, deer, and many others. The park also has a dedicated reptile house, showcasing a wide range of snakes, crocodiles, and other reptiles. The highlight of the park is the white tiger safari, where visitors can catch a glimpse of the rare and majestic white tigers. The park also has a children's park, a toy train ride, and a cafeteria, making it a perfect destination for a day out with family and friends. The park is home to over 80 species of animals, including endangered species like the Indian rhinoceros, Asiatic lion, and Himalayan black bear. Visitors can also see a variety of birds, reptiles, and small mammals. Tata Zoological Park offers a great opportunity to get up close with a wide range of animals, learn about conservation efforts, and enjoy a fun day out in nature.


Tata Zoo Lake

The Tata Zoological Park in Jamshedpur is a popular destination for nature lovers and animal enthusiasts. One of the major attractions of the park is the picturesque Tata Zoo Lake, which is spread across 3 hectares and is home to a variety of aquatic birds and animals. Visitors can enjoy boating facilities in the serene and scenic lake. The lake also serves as a source of freshwater for the animals in the zoo. The Tata Zoo Lake is surrounded by lush greenery and provides a peaceful and tranquil environment for visitors to relax and unwind amidst nature. The lake is situated in the heart of Tata Zoological Park and is a natural water body that was artificially enhanced and beautified by Tata Steel. The lake has an average depth of around 10 feet and is fed by a perennial stream that originates from the nearby Dalma Hills.
